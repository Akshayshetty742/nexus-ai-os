\# 🚀 Nexus AI – Student Life OS



\### 🧠 Tagline:



Your Multi-Agent "Digital Senior" for Academic \& Social Success.



\---



\## 📌 Project Overview



Nexus AI is an AI-powered dashboard designed to simplify student life by automating scheduling, tracking, and discovering opportunities.



It acts as a \*\*central command center\*\* where students can:



\* 📅 Plan their semester from syllabus

\* 🔁 Auto-manage missed tasks

\* 🏆 Discover hackathons \& events

\* 👥 Connect with local communities

\* ⚡ Build consistent study habits
